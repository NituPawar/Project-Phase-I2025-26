Curry Leaves (Murraya koenigii)

Curry Leaves are aromatic leaves widely used in Indian cooking for flavoring. Apart from culinary uses, they are also valued in Ayurveda for their medicinal properties and nutrients.

Uses

  • Commonly used in curries, chutneys, and soups.
  • Applied as herbal hair oil for strengthening hair.
  • Used in traditional remedies for stomach ailments.

Health Benefits

  • Improves digestion and reduces acidity.
  • Strengthens hair and prevents premature greying.
  • Supports weight loss and metabolism.
  • Controls blood sugar levels.
