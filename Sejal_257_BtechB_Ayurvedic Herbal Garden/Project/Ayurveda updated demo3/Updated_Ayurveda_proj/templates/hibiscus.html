Hibiscus (Hibiscus rosa-sinensis)

Hibiscus is a vibrant flowering plant known for its large blossoms and use in herbal teas, hair care, and traditional medicine.

Uses

  • Commonly brewed into hibiscus tea.
  • Applied in hair oils and shampoos for scalp health.
  • Used in skincare for its soothing properties.

Health Benefits

  • Helps reduce high blood pressure and cholesterol levels.
  • Rich in antioxidants that boost immunity.
  • Promotes healthy hair growth and reduces dandruff.
  • Supports liver health and detoxification.
