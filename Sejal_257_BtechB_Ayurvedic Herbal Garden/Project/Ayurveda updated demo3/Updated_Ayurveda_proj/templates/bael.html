Bael (Aegle marmelos)

Bael, also called Wood Apple, is a sacred fruit in India with numerous medicinal properties. Its pulp, leaves, and roots are all used in Ayurveda.

Uses

  • Fruit pulp eaten fresh or as sherbet.
  • Bael leaves offered in religious rituals.
  • Used in traditional remedies for stomach issues.

Health Benefits

  • Improves digestion and cures diarrhea.
  • Acts as a natural coolant in summer.
  • Boosts immunity and liver function.
  • Anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
