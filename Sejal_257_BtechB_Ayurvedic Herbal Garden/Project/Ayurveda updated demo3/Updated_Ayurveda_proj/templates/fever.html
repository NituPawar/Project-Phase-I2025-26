Fever & Cold Relief

Fever, cough, sneezing, and nasal congestion are often caused by a buildup of Kapha (mucus) and Ama (toxins) in the body. Ayurveda provides natural solutions to expel phlegm, boost immunity, and control body temperature without suppressing symptoms unnaturally.

1. Tulsi (Holy Basil)

  • Antiviral and antibacterial
  • Relieves cough and sore throat
  • Boosts respiratory immunity

Usage: Boil 5-6 Tulsi leaves with ginger and drink as herbal tea.

2. Giloy (Guduchi)

  • Reduces fever naturally
  • Strengthens immunity

Usage: Take 1–2 tsp Giloy juice twice a day or consume in tablet form.

3. Dry Ginger (Sunthi) & Honey

  • Breaks down mucus
  • Relieves chest congestion and chills

Usage: Mix 1/2 tsp dry ginger powder with honey and consume twice a day.

4. Steam Inhalation with Ajwain or Eucalyptus Oil

  • Clears nasal blockage
  • Relieves sinus pressure

Usage: Add Ajwain seeds or a few drops of eucalyptus oil in hot water and inhale steam.

5. Turmeric Milk (Haldi Doodh)

  • Natural anti-inflammatory
  • Enhances immunity

Usage: Mix 1/2 tsp turmeric powder in warm milk and drink at night.

6. Dietary Guidelines

  • Eat light, warm, easily digestible food like khichdi or soup
  • Avoid cold drinks, curd, and fried foods

7. Lifestyle Remedies

  • Take complete rest to support recovery
  • Do gentle gargles with warm salt water for sore throat relief
