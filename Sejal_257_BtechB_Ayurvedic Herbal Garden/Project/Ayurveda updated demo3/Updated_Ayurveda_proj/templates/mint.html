Mint (Mentha)

Mint is a fragrant herb commonly used in culinary dishes, beverages, and traditional medicine for its cooling and digestive properties.

Uses

  • Used in teas, salads, sauces, and desserts.
  • Applied in essential oils and cosmetics.
  • Used as a natural remedy for indigestion and nausea.

Health Benefits

  • Aids digestion and soothes the stomach.
  • Relieves headaches and respiratory congestion.
  • Provides antioxidants and supports immunity.
  • Freshens breath and promotes oral health.
