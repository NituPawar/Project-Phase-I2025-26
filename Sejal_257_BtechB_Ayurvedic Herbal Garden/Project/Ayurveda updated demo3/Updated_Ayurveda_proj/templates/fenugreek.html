Fenugreek (Methi)

Fenugreek (Methi) is a popular herb and spice used in cooking and traditional medicine. Both its seeds and leaves are valued for their unique flavor and medicinal qualities.

Uses

  • Seeds used in curries, pickles, and spice mixes.
  • Leaves (fresh or dried) used in cooking for flavor.
  • Used in herbal teas and home remedies.

Health Benefits

  • Helps control blood sugar levels, beneficial for diabetes.
  • Improves digestion and reduces acidity.
  • Boosts milk production in lactating mothers.
  • Rich in iron, aiding in anemia prevention.
