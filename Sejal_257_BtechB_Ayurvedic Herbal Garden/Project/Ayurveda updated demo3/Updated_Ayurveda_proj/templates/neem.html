Neem (Azadirachta indica)

Neem is known as the "village pharmacy" due to its wide range of medicinal properties. It has been used in Ayurveda for centuries.

Uses

  • Used in toothpaste, soaps, and cosmetics.
  • Applied in skin treatments for acne and infections.
  • Neem oil is used as a natural pesticide.

Health Benefits

  • Purifies blood and boosts immunity.
  • Treats skin conditions like acne and eczema.
  • Improves oral and dental health.
  • Controls dandruff and scalp issues.
