Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Hypertension is often caused by stress, poor diet, excess salt, sedentary lifestyle, and Vata-Pitta imbalance. Ayurveda focuses on calming the nervous system, improving circulation, and balancing doshas through herbs, diet, and relaxation practices.

1. Arjuna (Terminalia arjuna)

Properties: Cardioprotective, antioxidant, blood pressure regulating

  • Strengthens heart muscles
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Lowers blood pressure naturally

Usage: Take Arjuna powder with warm water or milk twice a day.

2. Sarpagandha (Rauwolfia serpentina)

  • Helps reduce high blood pressure
  • Calms the nervous system
  • Reduces anxiety and restlessness

Usage: Use under supervision in tablet or powder form.

3. Garlic (Lahsun)

  • Natural blood thinner
  • Improves heart health
  • Reduces cholesterol

Usage: Consume 1–2 raw garlic cloves in the morning or add to meals.

4. Dashmool

  • Balances Vata and relieves stress
  • Promotes better blood flow
  • Reduces inflammation

Usage: Take Dashmool decoction or powder with warm water.

5. Brahmi & Jatamansi

  • Reduces mental stress and anxiety
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Controls blood pressure fluctuations

Usage: Take in tablet or powder form with warm water.

6. Dietary Recommendations

  • Reduce salt intake
  • Eat fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Avoid oily, spicy, and processed food
  • Include cucumbers, bottle gourd, and pomegranate

7. Lifestyle & Yoga

  • Practice deep breathing, meditation, and Pranayama (Anulom Vilom, Bhramari)
  • Try light exercises and daily walks
  • Avoid anger, stress, and late-night sleep
