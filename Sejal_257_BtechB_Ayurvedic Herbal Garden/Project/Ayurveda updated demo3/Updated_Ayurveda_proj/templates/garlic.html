Garlic (Allium sativum)

Garlic is a widely used herb and spice, valued for its strong flavor and potent medicinal properties in traditional and modern medicine.

Uses

  • Used in cooking for flavor and seasoning.
  • Included in herbal remedies for colds and infections.
  • Consumed raw or as supplements for health benefits.

Health Benefits

  • Boosts immunity and fights common infections.
  • Helps lower blood pressure and cholesterol.
  • Improves heart health by reducing plaque buildup.
  • Contains antioxidants that protect against cell damage.
