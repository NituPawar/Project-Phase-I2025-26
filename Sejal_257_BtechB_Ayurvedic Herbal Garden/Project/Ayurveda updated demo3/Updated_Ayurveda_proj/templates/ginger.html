Ginger (Zingiber officinale)

Ginger is a rhizome widely used as a spice, flavoring agent, and traditional medicine for digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.

Uses

  • Used in cooking, teas, and beverages.
  • Applied in herbal remedies for nausea and colds.
  • Used in essential oils and cosmetics.

Health Benefits

  • Aids digestion and reduces nausea.
  • Reduces inflammation and joint pain.
  • Boosts immunity and respiratory health.
  • Supports cardiovascular health and circulation.
