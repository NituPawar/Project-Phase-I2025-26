Diabetes Management

Diabetes (Madhumeha) in Ayurveda is linked to impaired digestion, excess Kapha, and imbalance of Meda (fat tissue). Ayurvedic remedies help regulate blood sugar, enhance metabolism, and support pancreatic function naturally.

1. Jamun (Indian Blackberry)

  • Helps regulate blood sugar levels
  • Improves insulin sensitivity

Usage: Take Jamun seed powder with warm water once or twice a day.

2. Gurmar (Gymnema Sylvestre)

  • Reduces sugar absorption in the body
  • Supports pancreas function

Usage: Consume Gurmar powder or tablets before meals.

3. Fenugreek (Methi)

  • Rich in soluble fiber
  • Lowers blood sugar and improves metabolism

Usage: Soak 1 tsp methi seeds overnight and consume in the morning.

4. Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

  • Enhances pancreatic health
  • Rich in antioxidants and Vitamin C

Usage: Take Amla juice or powder with warm water daily.

5. Turmeric (Haldi)

  • Anti-inflammatory and antioxidant
  • Helps in balancing blood glucose levels

Usage: Consume turmeric milk or mix with warm water and black pepper.

6. Dietary Guidelines

  • Eat whole grains, green leafy vegetables, and fiber-rich foods
  • Avoid sugar, refined flour, and fried foods
  • Stay hydrated with warm water and herbal drinks

7. Lifestyle Practices

  • Practice yoga asanas like Vakrasana, Mandukasana, and Surya Namaskar
  • Do brisk walking or light exercise daily
  • Maintain a consistent sleep cycle
