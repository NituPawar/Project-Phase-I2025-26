Cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum)

Cinnamon is a spice obtained from the inner bark of trees from the genus Cinnamomum. It is valued for its warm flavor and medicinal properties.

Uses

  • Used in baking, desserts, and beverages.
  • Added to herbal teas and Ayurvedic remedies.
  • Used as a natural preservative and flavoring agent.

Health Benefits

  • Lowers blood sugar and improves insulin sensitivity.
  • Rich in antioxidants that reduce inflammation.
  • Improves heart health and circulation.
  • Fights bacterial and fungal infections.
