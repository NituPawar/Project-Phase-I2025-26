Aloe Vera (Aloe barbadensis miller)

Aloe Vera is a succulent plant known for its soothing gel, widely used in skincare and traditional medicine for its cooling and healing properties.

Uses

  • Applied on burns, wounds, and skin irritations.
  • Used in cosmetics and moisturizers.
  • Consumed in juices for health benefits.

Health Benefits

  • Heals skin burns and reduces scars.
  • Improves digestion and detoxifies the body.
  • Boosts immunity and reduces inflammation.
  • Supports oral and hair health.
