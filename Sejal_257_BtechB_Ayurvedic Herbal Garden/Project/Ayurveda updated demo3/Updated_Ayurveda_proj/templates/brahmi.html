Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri)

Brahmi is a medicinal herb used in Ayurveda for centuries, known to enhance brain function, memory, and overall wellness.

Uses

  • Consumed as herbal supplements or tonics.
  • Used in Ayurvedic oils for massage and relaxation.
  • Added to teas and herbal remedies for cognitive health.

Health Benefits

  • Improves memory and concentration.
  • Reduces anxiety and stress.
  • Supports brain health and cognitive function.
  • Has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
