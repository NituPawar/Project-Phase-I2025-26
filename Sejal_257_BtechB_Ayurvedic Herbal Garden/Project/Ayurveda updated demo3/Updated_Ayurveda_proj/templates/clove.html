Clove (Syzygium aromaticum)

Clove is a spice made from the flower buds of the clove tree. It has a strong aroma and is widely used in cooking and traditional medicine.

Uses

  • Used in curries, teas, and baked goods for flavor.
  • Oil used in dental care for toothache relief.
  • Ingredient in herbal remedies and Ayurvedic medicines.

Health Benefits

  • Provides relief from dental pain and bad breath.
  • Improves digestion and reduces bloating.
  • Contains antioxidants that protect against infections.
  • Supports liver health and blood sugar control.
