Sleep Disorders & Insomnia

Insomnia and disturbed sleep are often caused by aggravated Vata and Pitta doshas, excessive screen time, stress, irregular eating habits, or caffeine consumption. Ayurveda focuses on calming the nervous system, balancing bodily energies, and establishing a peaceful bedtime routine.

1. Ashwagandha (Withania Somnifera)

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Promotes deep and restful sleep
  • Strengthens the nervous system

Usage: Take Ashwagandha powder with warm milk before bed or use in capsule form.

2. Brahmi (Bacopa Monnieri)

  • Calms the mind and enhances cognitive function
  • Relieves mental fatigue and overthinking

Usage: Consume Brahmi tea or tablets in the evening.

3. Warm Milk with Nutmeg & Turmeric

  • Balances hormones and induces natural sleepiness
  • Improves digestion and relaxes the body

Usage: Add a pinch of nutmeg and turmeric to warm milk and drink 30 minutes before bed.

4. Oil Massage (Abhyanga)

  • Reduces restlessness and muscle tension
  • Promotes deep relaxation

Usage: Massage feet or scalp with warm sesame or coconut oil before bedtime.

5. Bedtime Herbal Tea

  • Use calming herbs like chamomile, tulsi, fennel, or licorice
  • Helps soothe the nervous system

6. Lifestyle & Sleep Hygiene

  • Maintain a fixed sleep schedule
  • Avoid screen exposure 1 hour before bed
  • Practice deep breathing or meditation
  • Keep the bedroom cool, dark, and quiet
