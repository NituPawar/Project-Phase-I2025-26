Bones & Joint Problems

Joint pain, stiffness, arthritis, osteoporosis, and inflammation arise due to imbalances in Vata dosha and depletion of bone tissue (Asthi Dhatu). Ayurveda offers natural remedies that nourish bones, reduce pain, improve flexibility, and promote long-term joint health.

1. Ashwagandha

Properties: Anti-inflammatory, strength-promoting, Vata-balancing

  • Reduces joint pain and swelling
  • Strengthens bones and muscles
  • Improves mobility and energy

Usage: Take 1 tsp Ashwagandha powder with warm milk at bedtime.

2. Guggul (Commiphora mukul)

Properties: Anti-arthritic, detoxifying, rejuvenating

  • Reduces inflammation and stiffness
  • Improves blood circulation to joints
  • Supports cartilage repair

Usage: Use Yograj Guggul or Mahayograj Guggul as per practitioner’s advice.

3. Hadjod (Cissus quadrangularis)

  • Promotes bone healing and density
  • Helps in fractures and osteoporosis
  • Strengthens connective tissue

Usage: Take Hadjod powder/tablet with warm water or milk.

4. Turmeric (Haldi) with Black Pepper

  • Powerful anti-inflammatory action
  • Reduces pain and stiffness
  • Supports joint lubrication

Usage: Drink golden milk (turmeric + milk + pinch of black pepper).

5. Sesame Oil (Til Taila) Massage

  • Lubricates joints
  • Reduces Vata and pain
  • Improves flexibility and warmth

Usage: Massage affected areas with warm sesame oil daily.

6. Moringa (Drumstick Leaves)

  • Rich in calcium, iron, and vitamins
  • Supports bone strength
  • Reduces inflammation

Usage: Consume moringa powder or fresh leaves in meals.

7. Dietary Recommendations

  • Include calcium-rich foods like sesame, ragi, leafy greens
  • Avoid cold, dry, and processed food
  • Drink warm soups and bone broth

8. Lifestyle & Yoga

  • Practice gentle yoga – Vrikshasana, Trikonasana, Bhujangasana
  • Avoid overexertion and cold exposure
  • Maintain regular movement to prevent stiffness
