Hair Fall & Scalp Disorders

Hair fall, dandruff, premature greying, and scalp infections often result from excess heat (Pitta), toxin buildup, hormonal imbalance, or lack of nutrition. Ayurveda focuses on internal detoxification and external nourishment using natural oils, herbs, and diet to strengthen hair roots and improve scalp health.

1. Bhringraj (False Daisy)

Properties: Hair growth promoter, cooling, rejuvenative

  • Strengthens hair follicles and reduces hair fall
  • Prevents premature greying
  • Improves scalp circulation

Usage: Apply Bhringraj oil on scalp before sleep or take Bhringraj powder with warm water.

2. Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

  • Rich in Vitamin C for hair nourishment
  • Reduces dandruff and dryness
  • Boosts collagen and hair thickness

Usage: Consume Amla juice daily or mix Amla powder with coconut oil and apply as a hair mask.

3. Neem (Azadirachta)

  • Anti-fungal and anti-bacterial for dandruff and scalp infections
  • Reduces itching and inflammation

Usage: Boil neem leaves in water and use as hair rinse or apply neem paste on scalp.

4. Fenugreek (Methi Seeds)

  • Prevents hair thinning and breakage
  • Conditions scalp naturally

Usage: Soak seeds overnight, grind into paste, and apply on scalp for 30 mins before washing.

5. Coconut Oil with Curry Leaves

  • Strengthens roots and promotes hair regrowth
  • Prevents premature greying

Usage: Boil curry leaves in coconut oil and massage scalp twice a week.

6. Dietary Guidelines

  • Include almonds, sesame seeds, jaggery, leafy greens
  • Avoid spicy, fried, and excessively salty food
  • Stay hydrated and consume ghee for nourishment

7. Lifestyle Remedies

  • Reduce stress with yoga, pranayama, and scalp massage
  • Avoid chemical shampoos and heat styling
  • Sleep well to maintain hormonal balance
