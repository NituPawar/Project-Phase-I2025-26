Shatavari (Asparagus racemosus)

Shatavari is a renowned Ayurvedic herb known as the "Queen of Herbs" for women. It supports reproductive health, vitality, and overall wellness.

Uses

  • Consumed as powder, tablets, or tonics.
  • Used in Ayurvedic formulations for women’s health.
  • Supports lactation and fertility naturally.

Health Benefits

  • Boosts immunity and vitality.
  • Balances hormones and supports reproductive health.
  • Relieves stress and improves energy.
  • Acts as an adaptogen to fight fatigue.
