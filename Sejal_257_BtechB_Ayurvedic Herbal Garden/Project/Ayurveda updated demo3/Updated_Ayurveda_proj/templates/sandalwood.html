Sandalwood (Santalum album)

Sandalwood is a fragrant wood valued for its essential oil, widely used in Ayurveda, cosmetics, and spirituality. Its cooling properties make it highly beneficial in traditional medicine.

Uses

  • Used in perfumes, incense, and cosmetics.
  • Applied as sandalwood paste for skincare.
  • Used in Ayurvedic and Unani medicines.

Health Benefits

  • Soothes skin irritation and acne.
  • Reduces stress and promotes relaxation.
  • Improves focus and mental clarity.
  • Anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
