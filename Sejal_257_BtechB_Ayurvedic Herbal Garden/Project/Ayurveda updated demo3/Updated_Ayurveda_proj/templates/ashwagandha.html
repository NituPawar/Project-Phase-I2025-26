Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)

Ashwagandha is a popular herb in Ayurvedic medicine, known for its adaptogenic properties that help the body manage stress and improve vitality.

Uses

  • Used in powders, capsules, and herbal teas.
  • Applied in traditional remedies for energy and vitality.
  • Used as a natural supplement for stress management.

Health Benefits

  • Reduces stress and anxiety.
  • Enhances stamina and physical performance.
  • Boosts immunity and brain function.
  • Supports hormonal balance and overall wellness.
