Turmeric (Curcuma longa)

Turmeric is a golden-yellow spice widely used in cooking and Ayurvedic medicine. It contains the active compound curcumin, which has powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Uses

  • Used as a spice in cooking (curries, milk, teas).
  • Applied in skincare for healing and brightening.
  • Used as a natural coloring agent.

Health Benefits

  • Reduces inflammation and joint pain.
  • Boosts immunity and digestion.
  • Promotes wound healing and skin health.
  • Supports liver function and detoxification.
