Black Pepper (Piper nigrum)

Black Pepper, known as the "king of spices," is a flowering vine cultivated for its fruit (peppercorns), which is dried and used as a spice and seasoning.

Uses

  • Widely used in cooking for flavor enhancement.
  • Added to traditional medicines and herbal remedies.
  • Used in Ayurveda for balancing digestion.

Health Benefits

  • Improves digestion and metabolism.
  • Contains antioxidants that protect against free radicals.
  • Enhances nutrient absorption, especially curcumin from turmeric.
  • Supports respiratory health by relieving congestion.
