Pregnancy & Women’s Wellness – Ayurvedic Support

Ayurveda offers gentle and natural support for women during pregnancy and throughout life. It focuses on nourishment, hormonal balance, emotional stability, and strength through herbs, diet, routines, and lifestyle practices.

1. Shatavari

  • Supports reproductive health
  • Promotes hormonal balance
  • Boosts fertility and lactation

Usage: Take Shatavari powder with warm milk or ghee.

2. Ashoka

  • Helps regulate menstrual cycles
  • Reduces cramps and discomfort
  • Supports uterine health

Usage: Use Ashoka churna or syrup under guidance.

3. Fenugreek (Methi)

  • Supports lactation post-delivery
  • Improves digestion and energy

Usage: Consume soaked methi seeds or methi tea.

4. Ginger (Adrak)

  • Helps with morning sickness and nausea
  • Improves digestion

Usage: Drink ginger tea or add to meals.

5. Fennel Seeds (Saunf)

  • Reduces bloating and acidity
  • Promotes milk production

Usage: Chew fennel seeds or drink fennel tea.

6. Prenatal Diet Recommendations

  • Eat warm, fresh, and nourishing foods
  • Include milk, ghee, dates, almonds, moong dal, and leafy greens
  • Avoid spicy, stale, and processed foods

7. Lifestyle & Yoga

  • Practice gentle prenatal yoga: Cat-Cow, Butterfly, and Vajrasana
  • Do slow breathing and meditation
  • Get adequate sleep and stay hydrated
  • Avoid stress and excessive physical strain
