Tulip Ginger (Curcuma zedoaria)

Tulip Ginger, also known as Zedoary, is a medicinal rhizome related to turmeric. It has a slightly bitter taste and is used in traditional medicines across Asia.

Uses

  • Used as a spice and food flavoring.
  • Ingredient in herbal tonics for digestion.
  • Applied in traditional medicine for inflammation.

Health Benefits

  • Aids digestion and reduces bloating.
  • Relieves pain and inflammation.
  • Boosts immunity and fights infections.
  • Supports liver detoxification.
