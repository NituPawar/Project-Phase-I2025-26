Lemon (Citrus limon)

Lemon is a citrus fruit rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. It is widely used in culinary dishes, beverages, and traditional medicine for its refreshing flavor and health-promoting properties.

Uses

  • Used in juices, teas, and culinary dishes.
  • Applied in skincare for brightening and cleansing.
  • Used as a natural household cleaner.

Health Benefits

  • Boosts immunity and protects against infections.
  • Aids digestion and detoxification.
  • Promotes healthy skin and reduces acne.
  • Supports heart health and reduces blood pressure.
