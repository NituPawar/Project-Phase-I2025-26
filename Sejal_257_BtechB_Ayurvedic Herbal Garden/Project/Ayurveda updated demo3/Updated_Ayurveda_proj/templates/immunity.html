General Immunity Boost – Ayurvedic Remedies

Ayurveda emphasizes strengthening the immune system (Ojas) to prevent diseases. Herbs, diet, and lifestyle practices help enhance natural immunity, balance doshas, reduce inflammation, and improve overall vitality.

1. Giloy (Guduchi)

  • Boosts immunity and reduces infections
  • Detoxifies the body and balances all three doshas
  • Supports liver and digestive health

Usage: Take Giloy juice or tablets daily with warm water.

2. Ashwagandha

  • Strengthens immunity and energy levels
  • Reduces stress and cortisol levels
  • Improves endurance and overall health

Usage: Take 1 tsp Ashwagandha powder with warm milk at bedtime.

3. Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

  • Rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants
  • Enhances white blood cell activity
  • Supports tissue repair and detoxification

Usage: Consume fresh Amla, juice, or powder daily.

4. Turmeric (Haridra)

  • Anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties
  • Supports immune system function
  • Helps fight infections naturally

Usage: Mix ½ tsp turmeric in warm milk or water daily.

5. Tulsi (Holy Basil)

  • Boosts respiratory immunity
  • Reduces stress and enhances vitality
  • Fights microbial infections

Usage: Drink Tulsi tea or chew fresh leaves regularly.

6. Dietary Recommendations

  • Include fresh fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, and whole grains
  • Drink herbal teas and warm water throughout the day
  • Avoid processed, fried, and excessively sugary foods

7. Lifestyle & Yoga

  • Practice daily Pranayama like Anulom Vilom and Kapalabhati
  • Get adequate sleep and manage stress
  • Do light exercises or yoga to improve circulation and immunity
  • Meditation and mindful practices help strengthen body and mind
