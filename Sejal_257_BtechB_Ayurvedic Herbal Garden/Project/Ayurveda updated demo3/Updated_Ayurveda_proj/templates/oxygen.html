Respiratory Issues

Respiratory problems like cough, asthma, bronchitis, nasal congestion, and allergies often result from Kapha imbalance, weak immunity, or exposure to pollutants. Ayurveda uses herbs, steam therapies, and lifestyle practices to clear mucus, strengthen lungs, and enhance respiratory function.

1. Tulsi (Holy Basil)

  • Natural expectorant and bronchodilator
  • Relieves cough, cold, and congestion
  • Boosts lung immunity

Usage: Drink tulsi tea or chew fresh leaves daily.

2. Mulethi (Licorice Root)

  • Soothes throat irritation and dry cough
  • Reduces inflammation in airways
  • Helps in bronchitis and asthma

Usage: Boil mulethi powder in water or consume with honey.

3. Ginger (Adrak / Sunthi)

  • Anti-inflammatory and decongestant
  • Breaks down mucus and clears air passages
  • Improves digestion and immunity

Usage: Drink ginger tea with honey or add to meals.

4. Vasaka (Adhatoda Vasica)

  • Effective in asthma and bronchial disorders
  • Acts as an expectorant and bronchodilator
  • Clears respiratory tract of phlegm

Usage: Take vasaka syrup or powder with warm water.

5. Turmeric (Haldi)

  • Anti-allergic and antimicrobial
  • Helps clear nasal and chest congestion
  • Boosts immunity and lung health

Usage: Take turmeric milk or mix haldi with honey.

6. Dietary & Lifestyle Guidelines

  • Drink warm water and herbal teas
  • Avoid cold, fried, and dairy-heavy foods
  • Include spices like black pepper, cinnamon, and cloves
  • Use steam inhalation with eucalyptus or mint oil

7. Breathing & Yoga Practices

  • Practice Pranayamas like Anulom Vilom, Bhastrika, and Kapalbhati
  • Do yoga poses like Bhujangasana, Matsyasana, and Dhanurasana
  • Stay away from dust, smoke, and cold air exposure
