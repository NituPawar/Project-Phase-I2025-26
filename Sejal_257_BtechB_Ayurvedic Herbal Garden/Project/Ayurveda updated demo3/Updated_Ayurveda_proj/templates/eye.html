Eye Health & Vision Support

Maintaining eye health is crucial as modern lifestyle and screen exposure can strain eyes, cause dryness, blurred vision, and early onset of cataracts. Ayurveda recommends herbs, dietary adjustments, and eye exercises to improve vision, nourish eye tissues, and reduce eye strain.

1. Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

  • Rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants
  • Reduces oxidative stress in eye tissues
  • Prevents cataract formation and improves vision

Usage: Consume fresh amla, amla juice, or amla powder with warm water daily.

2. Triphala

  • Balances Pitta dosha and strengthens eyes
  • Reduces eye strain and improves night vision
  • Supports detoxification of eye tissues

Usage: Take 1 tsp Triphala powder with warm water at bedtime; Triphala eye drops can be used after consultation.

3. Bilberry

  • Enhances visual acuity and night vision
  • Supports retinal health and blood circulation to the eyes
  • Reduces eye fatigue and redness

Usage: Take bilberry supplements or juice under guidance.

4. Fennel Seeds (Saunf)

  • Reduces eye irritation and dryness
  • Acts as a natural coolant for eyes
  • Supports overall eye comfort

Usage: Boil 1 tsp fennel seeds in water, cool, and wash eyes (after expert guidance) or consume fennel tea.

5. Dietary & Lifestyle Guidelines

  • Eat fresh fruits and green leafy vegetables
  • Include carrots, spinach, and pumpkin rich in beta-carotene
  • Avoid excessive screen time and exposure to bright light
  • Drink plenty of water to maintain eye moisture

6. Eye Exercises & Yoga

  • Palming: Rub hands and place over closed eyes to relax them
  • Trataka (concentrated gazing) for focus and eye strength
  • Yoga poses: Trikonasana, Shavasana, and gentle neck stretches
