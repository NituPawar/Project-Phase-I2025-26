Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

Amla, also known as Indian Gooseberry, is a rich source of vitamin C and powerful antioxidants. It is widely used in Ayurvedic medicine for rejuvenation and overall wellness.

Uses

  • Consumed raw, in juices, or as dried powder.
  • Used in Ayurvedic formulations like Chyawanprash and Triphala.
  • Applied in hair oils and skincare products.

Health Benefits

  • Boosts immunity and fights infections.
  • Promotes healthy skin and delays aging.
  • Enhances digestion and metabolism.
  • Strengthens hair and improves vision.
