Tulsi (Ocimum sanctum)

Tulsi, also known as Holy Basil, is considered sacred in India and widely used in Ayurveda for its spiritual and medicinal properties.

Uses

  • Used in herbal teas and Ayurvedic remedies.
  • Applied in oils for stress relief and immunity.
  • Worshipped in households for its sacred value.

Health Benefits

  • Boosts immunity and protects against infections.
  • Reduces stress and anxiety.
  • Improves respiratory health.
  • Acts as a natural detoxifier.
